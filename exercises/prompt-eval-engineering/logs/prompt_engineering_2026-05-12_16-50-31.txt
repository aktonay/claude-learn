======================================================================
  KEY TAKEAWAYS
======================================================================

  1. BE CLEAR AND DIRECT: Start with a simple, clear ask.
     This is your baseline. Low scores are expected.

  2. BE SPECIFIC: Add guidelines for format, content, and detail level.
     This typically produces the LARGEST score improvement.

  3. USE XML TAGS: Structure your prompt with clear boundaries.
     Impact grows with prompt complexity and data volume.

  4. PROVIDE EXAMPLES: Show, don't tell.
     One good example calibrates format, tone, and detail better
     than paragraphs of instructions.

  measurable. You see EXACTLY how much each step improved scores.
